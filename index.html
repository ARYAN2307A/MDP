Comprehensive Safety Features

👁️

Advanced Drowsiness Tracking (EAR)

Real-time Eye Aspect Ratio (EAR) computation tracks 6 eye coordinates at 30fps. Drops below the 0.25 threshold for 10 frames instantly trigger auditory micro-sleep alarms.

👄

Yawn & Fatigue Detection (MAR)

Monitors Mouth Aspect Ratio (MAR) to predict exhaustion. Calculates vertical and horizontal lip distances to distinguish genuine yawns from talking, actively adjusting your Safety Score.

🧠

Emotion & Mental Health Monitor

Powered by EfficientNet-B0 to analyze facial expressions in real-time. Detects aggressive driving states, logging events and triggering High Stress alerts when anger exceeds 80%.

🚨

Location-Aware SOS Integration

If the driver becomes unresponsive for 4 continuous seconds, an automated Twilio SMS is dispatched to emergency contacts, complete with an approximate live Google Maps link.

⚙️

Hardware Automation (Arduino)

Bridges AI vision directly to physical indicators. The Python backend communicates over USB to an Arduino microcontroller, triggering live LED status lights or steering wheel haptics.

📊

Live Safety Dashboard

A sophisticated web interface acting as an active "Black Box." Features live video relay, dynamic 0-100 Safety Score tracking, and a chronological log of all driving events.

The Technology Pipeline: How It Works

01

Face & Landmark Detection

Captures live video via OpenCV and uses Dlib to instantly pinpoint 68 specific facial landmarks.

02

Mathematical Ratio Extraction

Calculates Euclidean distances between eyelids and lips to constantly extract Eye and Mouth Aspect Ratios.

03

Emotional Processing Pipeline

Feeds the face image into the EfficientNet-B0 neural network to analyze and output live emotional states.

04

Threshold Logic & Event Dispatch

Evaluates metrics against thresholds to trigger alarms, Arduino hardware, and location-aware Twilio SOS alerts.

About SafelyDriven AI

SafelyDriven is a comprehensive, open-source driver monitoring ecosystem engineered to actively combat the leading causes of traffic accidents: fatigue, micro-sleeps, and emotional volatility (road rage/stress). Modern vehicles often rely on steering wheel telemetry to guess if a driver is tired; SafelyDriven brings direct, zero-contact biometric observation to any vehicle with a simple webcam.


Built on a robust Python backend using Flask, OpenCV, Dlib, and Scipy, the system achieves near-instantaneous inference times even on standard CPU hardware. By bridging advanced Computer Vision (facial landmarks) with Deep Learning heuristics (EfficientNet-B0), and extending out to Physical Hardware (Arduino) and Cloud Telecom (Twilio API), SafelyDriven represents the future of accessible, retroactive vehicular safety technology.
